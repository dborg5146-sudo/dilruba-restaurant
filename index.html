A Hidden Gem in Rugby

Welcome to Dilruba, a beloved local restaurant serving the heart of Amritsar right here in Rugby. Every dish is cooked fresh daily – nothing frozen, nothing microwaved. From smoky tandoori chicken to rich butter curries and fluffy naan, we bring you the true taste of Punjab with warm, personal service.

Vegetarian? No problem – we have a large vegetarian selection clearly marked on our menu. Dine in, take away, or order via Uber Eats. We promise a meal you won’t forget.
